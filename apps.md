# My Apps

Here are some applications I've built that you might find useful:

## [Pixel Perfect](pixelperfect.html)

An intuitive online tool for resizing, converting, and optimizing images. Perfect for quickly adjusting images for social media, websites, or other projects.

## [Dead Man's Switch](deadmanswitch.html)

A security application that performs predefined actions if you don't check in for a specified period of time.

## [Shortz.dev](shortz.dev.html)

A URL shortener service that provides analytics and custom short links for your long URLs.

## [Text2Mermaid](text2mermaid.html)

A tool that converts plain text descriptions into Mermaid diagrams for visualization of workflows, sequences, and more.
